Menu FAQs

Do you have a lunch special?

Yes — weekdays 11:00am–2:00pm with options like a classic square, rotating slice of the day, or slice + half salad.

Do you sell pizza by the slice?

Yes — Monday–Friday 11:00am–2:00pm. The slice of the day changes so you can try something new.
