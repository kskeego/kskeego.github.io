About Woodward Pizza

At Woodward Pizza, we believe every slice tells a story. Ours began with a love for Detroit‑style pizza and the dream of bringing its bold, crispy‑edged flavor to Edmond and the Oklahoma City metro. From the very first bake, our goal has been simple: make the best pizza in Oklahoma, serve it with pride, and create a place where the community feels at home.

The Detroit‑Style Tradition

Detroit‑style pizza is famous for its square shape, caramelized “frico” edge, and a crisp‑bottomed, airy crumb. It’s baked in seasoned steel pans, a technique that gives every slice its signature texture. While many pizzerias serve thin or New York‑style pies, we wanted to introduce the OKC metro to true Detroit‑style pizza.

Our Story in Edmond

We opened in downtown Edmond’s Icehouse Project to be more than a pizza shop—a welcoming space for neighbors, families after ballgames, and friends out for a night with great food and drinks. Today we’re a go‑to spot for Edmond, Nichols Hills, The Village, and Oklahoma City guests.

Ingredients and Craft

Every pizza starts with dough made fresh daily for a balance of chew and crispness. We top it with bold flavors and finish with bright stripes of our signature red sauce. Our team rotates seasonal features—from smoky BBQ pies to veggie‑packed combinations—so there’s always something new.

We also offer vegan cheese and vegetarian pizzas so families with different preferences can enjoy the menu together.

Beyond Pizza — Bar and Gelato

Our full bar features craft cocktails and local beer; our patio is the place to be on warm evenings. Cap it off with freshly churned gelato and you’ve got dinner done right.

Community Connection

We host after‑game celebrations, team dinners, and neighborhood meet‑ups. As a locally owned Edmond pizzeria, giving back to the community that supports us is part of who we are.
