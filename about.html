About Woodward Pizza

We brought authentic Detroit‑style pizza to Edmond and the greater Oklahoma City metro. Our steel pans create the signature crispy, caramelized edge (frico) and airy crumb that OKC pizza lovers crave.

Why Edmond & OKC Love Detroit‑Style

It’s the best of both worlds: a crunchy, cheesy edge with a tender interior, finished with bold sauce. Our Tuesday “Not NY” rounds keep things fun and different.

Beyond Pizza

We’re more than the best pizza—enjoy a full bar, patio seating for summer nights, and freshly churned gelato to finish strong.
