About Woodward Pizza

At Woodward Pizza, we believe every slice tells a story. Ours began with a love for Detroit-style pizza and the dream of bringing its bold, crispy-edged flavor to Edmond and the Oklahoma City metro. From the very first bake, our goal has been simple: make the best pizza in Oklahoma, serve it with pride, and create a place where the community feels at home.

The Detroit-Style Tradition

Detroit-style pizza is famous for its square shape, thick yet airy crust, and caramelized cheese “frico” around the edges. It’s baked in seasoned steel pans, a technique that gives every slice its signature crisp bottom and chewy interior. While many pizzerias serve New York or thin-crust pies, we set out to introduce the OKC metro to true Detroit-style pizza. Guests often tell us it’s unlike anything they’ve ever tasted locally.

Our Story in Edmond

Woodward Pizza opened its doors in the Icehouse Project in downtown Edmond, a space designed for gathering. We wanted more than just a pizza shop — we wanted a place where neighbors could connect, families could relax after a ballgame, and friends could enjoy a night out with great food and drinks. Over time, our restaurant has become a go-to spot not only for Edmond residents but also for people driving in from Oklahoma City, Nichols Hills, The Village, and beyond.

Ingredients and Craft

Every pizza starts with dough made fresh daily, giving our crust the perfect balance of chew and crispness. We layer mozzarella right to the edge for that caramelized frico Detroit-style is known for, then finish with warm sauce after the bake. Our team is always creating new specials and rotating features — from smoky BBQ pies to veggie-packed combinations — so there’s always something new to discover.

And because we know not everyone eats the same way, we also offer vegan cheese and vegetarian pizzas. Families and groups with dietary restrictions can enjoy our menu together without compromise.

Beyond Pizza — Bar and Gelato

Woodward Pizza isn’t just about the pies. Our full bar features craft cocktails and seasonal drinks that pair perfectly with Detroit-style squares. In the summer, our patio is a favorite hangout spot, complete with misters to keep things cool. And no meal is complete without dessert — our rotating gelato (including dairy-free flavors) is a guest favorite.

Community Connection

Supporting the community is part of who we are. We host after-game celebrations for local schools, team dinners, and casual neighborhood meet-ups. Many families have made Woodward Pizza part of their weekly tradition. We’re proud to be a locally owned Edmond pizzeria that gives back to the community that supports us.

Why Choose Woodward Pizza?

  • Authentic Detroit-style pizza baked in steel pans
  • Locally owned and operated in Edmond, Oklahoma
  • Serving guests from across the OKC metro
  • Full bar, patio seating, and rotating gelato
  • Vegan and vegetarian-friendly menu options

Whether you’re a lifelong Edmond resident or visiting from Oklahoma City, Nichols Hills, or The Village, we invite you to stop by and taste why Woodward Pizza is quickly becoming known as the best pizza in Oklahoma. Dine in, order online, or grab a drink on the patio — we’ll save you a square.
