Visit FAQs

Where can I park?

Shared lot by the building, adjoining street parking, and the Farmers Market parking a short walk away. Even when the lot is full, we have plenty of patio seating.

Do you take reservations?

We’re a small community space and stay first‑come, first‑served. For larger groups, we can connect you with The Stables at Icehouse next door and provide catering.
