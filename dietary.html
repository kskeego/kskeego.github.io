Dietary Options

Vegan Pizza

Try our Vegan Cheese Pizza made with dairy‑free mozzarella and signature red sauce. Customize with plant‑based toppings.

Dairy‑Free Options

Enjoy Detroit‑style or “Not NY” rounds with our dairy‑free cheese substitute.

Vegetarian Choices

From our classic Veggie Detroit‑Style to build‑your‑own options, vegetarians have plenty to enjoy.

Gluten Sensitivity

We don’t currently offer a gluten‑free crust. Our kitchen uses wheat, dairy, nuts, and other allergens; cross‑contact is possible.

Dietary FAQs

Is your vegan cheese dairy‑free? Yes—our vegan cheese is dairy‑free. Let us know if you’d like to avoid cross‑contact and we’ll do our best.

Gluten‑free? We don’t currently offer a gluten‑free crust. Our kitchen handles wheat and other allergens, so cross‑contact is possible.

Customization tips: Many pizzas can be made vegetarian or dairy‑free. Ask our team or note your preferences in online ordering.

⚠️ We cannot guarantee allergen‑free meals, but we’ll do our best to accommodate.
