Dietary Options

Vegan

We never want anyone to feel left out because of their diet. We offer vegan cheese, plant‑based meats, and fresh in‑house cut veggies. Look for our occasional Vegan Pizza of the Month.

Dairy‑Free

We offer dairy‑free cheese and can customize many pizzas so everyone at the table can enjoy a great slice together. (Our daughter had a minor dairy allergy as a newborn — we get it.)

Gluten‑Free

We spent 9+ months developing a gluten‑free dough that’s light, fluffy, crisp, and deep — designed to be almost indistinguishable from our standard Detroit‑style pizza. We use a separate mixer and pans, and we make GF dough later in the day so flour dust has time to settle. While we can’t guarantee a flour‑free environment, we do our very best to minimize cross‑contact.

Allergen Note

Our kitchen handles wheat, dairy, eggs, nuts, and soy. Please tell us about any allergies so we can do our best to accommodate.
